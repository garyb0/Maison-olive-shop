# Chez Olive - Google Play Listing EN-CA

## Name

Chez Olive

## Short Description

Local pet shop: Rimouski delivery, orders, support, and dog QR profiles.

## Full Description

Chez Olive brings the local shop, order tracking, human support, and dog QR profiles into one app.

Built for pet families in Rimouski and nearby areas, the Chez Olive app helps you order essentials, follow delivery details, and keep important companion information close.

With the Chez Olive customer app, you can:

- browse the shop;
- prepare a cart for local delivery;
- find your orders;
- follow delivery information;
- manage your account and addresses;
- contact Chez Olive support;
- open your dog QR profiles;
- receive useful notifications if you enable them.

The promise is simple: carefully chosen products, low-friction local delivery, and QR profiles that can help when timing matters.
